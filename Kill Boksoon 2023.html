KILL BOKSOON

Gil Bok-Soon is a single mother and merciless murderer who works for Cha Min-Kyu's M.K. Ent. Before renewing her contract, Bok-Soon engages in a kill-or-be-killed confrontation.

Synopsis

Gil Bok-Soon (Jeon Do-yeon) is a contract killer working for M. K. Ent. Highly regarded by her peers, she has a 100% success rate and is one of a few killers rated "A" by her company. She is also a single mother to Jae-yeong (Kim Si-a), a closeted teenager who studies at a prestigious private school. M. K. Ent. is run by Cha Min-Kyu (Sol Kyung-gu) - a powerful and experienced killer, who holds much respect for Bok-Soon; and by his younger sister, Cha Min-Hee (Esom), who despises her. Bok-Soon is sexually involved with a co-worker, Han Hee-Sung (Koo Kyo-hwan) - a skilled killer, whose career progress was thwarted for unknown reasons.
